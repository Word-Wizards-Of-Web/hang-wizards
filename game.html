codeHang

How to play:

codeHang is a word-guessing game where you try to guess a hidden word. The word is represented by boxes, and you will have 10 attempts to fill in the blanks by proposing 1 letter at a time. The game continues until the word is guessed correctly or you run out of guesses.

Note: All hidden words are related to HTML, CSS and JavaScript.
